The Best Books I have Read

My Top Three Best Books Of All Times

The Cashflow Quadrant

The Cashflow Quadrant was written by RObert Kowaski and is q guide to financial freedom. it's the second book in the rich Dad series and it reveals how certain persons work less, earn more, pay less in taxes and learn to become financially free.

The Intelligent Investor

The Intelligent Investor written by Benjamin Graham is out to help one understand the value of the business you are investing in, in order to make investments objectively, prioritise research over impulses and most importantly to understand that sucessful investing requires patience and discipline

Soul Land: The Legend Of The Dragon King

Soul Land is a Chinese fictional novel written by Tang Jia San Shao. It has about 1487 chapters. the book illustrates the journey of a soul master by name Tang Wullin who started off with a defective Soul Spirit but due to his hardwork, resilence and dedication as well as, some hidden power within his bloodline, he became one of the most powerful soul masters.
